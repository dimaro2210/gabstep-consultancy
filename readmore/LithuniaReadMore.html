Lithuania Study Visa

Lithuania offers high-quality European education at affordable prices in a rapidly growing economy. Our Lithuania Study Visa service provides comprehensive support for students seeking to study in this innovative Baltic nation.

Why Study in Lithuania?

  • Internationally recognized degrees meeting EU standards
  • Affordable tuition fees and lower cost of living than Western Europe
  • Growing number of English-taught programs across disciplines
  • Safe country with excellent quality of life and rich cultural heritage
  • Strategic location in the EU with easy travel throughout Europe

Our Services Include:

  • University selection based on academic background and career goals
  • Application assistance for Lithuanian universities
  • Guidance on required documents and academic qualifications
  • Visa application support and interview preparation
  • Financial documentation assistance and proof of funds guidance
  • Pre-departure orientation and accommodation support

Eligibility Requirements:

To qualify for a Lithuanian study visa, you must:

  • Have admission to an accredited Lithuanian educational institution
  • Demonstrate sufficient financial means (€400/month minimum)
  • Show proof of English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL) for English programs
  • Have comprehensive health insurance coverage
  • Provide a clean criminal record certificate

Post-Study Opportunities

After graduation, international students can extend their residence permit for 6 months to seek employment. Lithuania offers opportunities in growing sectors like IT, engineering, and finance.
